# Use Cases

## 1. Remote personal operations
Use Telegram to interact with a personal AI assistant while away from the machine.

## 2. Workflow launching
Open URLs, start local processes, or prepare working environments remotely.

## 3. File and desktop management
Organize files, build folders, prepare structured workspaces, and reduce repetitive setup work.

## 4. Business consulting support
Create business audit workspaces, research local businesses, prepare client-facing and internal outputs, and support consulting workflows.

## 5. Creative support
Build and iterate on custom FL Studio startup experiences, prepare media workflows, and coordinate creative system tasks.

## 6. Monitoring and information systems
Support local information-monitoring workflows such as civic watch setups, recurring research systems, and structured summaries.
